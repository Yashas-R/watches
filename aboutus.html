Our Story · Est. Geneva 1977

Crafting eternity,
one timepiece at a time.

Founded in Geneva in 1977 by master watchmaker Henri Leclerc, Horologium was born from a singular obsession: to create watches that transcend utility and become heirlooms for future generations.

Every watch in our atelier passes through the hands of at least twelve specialists before reaching a collector. We spend an average of 340 hours on each piece — not because we must, but because excellence demands nothing less.

Today, Horologium watches reside in private collections across 18 countries, worn by those who understand that true luxury is measured in the depth of craft behind each tick.

Our Journey

Four decades of excellence

1977
Founded in Geneva
Henri Leclerc establishes Horologium SA on Rue du Rhone with four watchmakers and a vision to challenge established maisons.
1983
First In-House Calibre
Cal. H-001 unveiled — hand-wound movement with 52-hour power reserve. Wins Grand Prix d'Horlogerie de Geneve.
1992
Perpetuel Classique Launched
Our perpetual calendar complication debuts, recognised as one of the finest of its generation.
2001
Expansion to Asia & Middle East
First boutiques open in Tokyo and Dubai, bringing the Geneva atelier experience to collectors worldwide.
2012
Heritage Tourbillon Debuts
Flying tourbillon at 6 o'clock with 78-hour power reserve — cementing Horologium among the great complication makers.
2020
Cosmos Orrery — 12 Pieces Only
Miniature solar system on the wrist. The most complex watch Horologium has ever produced. All 12 placed within weeks.
2025
India Flagship Launch
We bring the full Horologium experience to Indian collectors with white-glove delivery and lifetime service across India.
